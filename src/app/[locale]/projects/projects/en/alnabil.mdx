## Overview

Development of the official website for Alnabil for Wood Industries (شركة النبيل للصناعات الخشبية), a leading importer and supplier of high-quality timber in Libya. The company serves workshops, contractors, and designers by providing premium wood types suited for construction, furniture, and decoration projects.

## Technologies and Features

### Product Catalog

A structured showcase of wood types and products, including red wood, white wood, beech, okoume, treated wood, wood panels, MDF, melamine boards, and plywood—each with clear descriptions for construction, furniture, and industrial applications.

### Contact and Location

Integration of contact details, working hours (8:00 AM – 5:00 PM, Saturday–Thursday), and location information for the Tagura, Tripoli branch, making it easy for contractors and designers to reach the company.

### Professional Branding

A design that reflects the company's expertise and reliability in the wood industry, with a focus on trust and professionalism for B2B clients.

### Responsive and Performant

A fast, mobile-friendly site optimized for contractors and professionals browsing on the go.

## Outcome

Alnabil's website establishes a professional digital presence that showcases the company's wood range and facilitates contact with contractors, designers, and workshops across Libya.
