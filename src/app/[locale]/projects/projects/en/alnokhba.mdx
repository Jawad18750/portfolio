## Overview

Development of the official website for Al Nokhba Al Modeeah (النخبة المضيئة), a Libyan company specialized in importing and distributing high-quality timber from the world's best sources. The company provides wood products for construction, manufacturing, and decoration, with a strict commitment to quality and reliability in every shipment. Their slogan: "We distribute timber the way it should be."

## Technologies and Features

### Timber Import

Showcase of timber sourced from trusted global suppliers in Sweden, Austria, Gabon, and Finland, with strict quality control on every shipment.

### Distribution and Supply

An efficient distribution network covering most regions of Libya through branches in Zliten (headquarters), Tripoli (Tajoura), and Benghazi (Al Hilal Market), ensuring timely delivery for project needs.

### Support for Projects and Traders

Serving carpentry workshops, contractors, and furniture factories with customized quantities and competitive prices, plus flexible supply according to client requirements.

### Become a Supplier / Distributor

Dedicated sections for international suppliers and local distributors to connect with the company, expanding the timber supply chain in Libya.

### Articles and Insights

A blog section sharing expertise on timber—from sourcing in Northern European forests to quality control and treated wood—keeping contractors and traders informed.

### Location and Contact

Integration of branch locations with maps (Zliten, Tripoli, Benghazi), contact details, and clear calls-to-action for ordering and inquiries.

## Outcome

Al Nokhba Al Modeeah's website establishes a professional digital presence for timber import and distribution in Libya, connecting contractors, workshops, and factories with high-quality wood from trusted international sources.
