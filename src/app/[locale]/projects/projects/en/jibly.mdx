## Overview

**Jibly** is a multi-vendor commerce and delivery platform built for **entrepreneur Nizar Balagha** in **Al-Jamil**, with the goal of connecting customers, stores, and drivers through one unified digital system.

Rather than shipping a standalone app, we designed Jibly as an integrated platform — multiple connected applications plus a central admin panel — shaped around Libyan market needs and the day-to-day operations of stores and delivery teams.

Today, Jibly is **live on the App Store and Google Play**, serving real users in Al-Jamil.

## The Challenge

The core challenge was building one platform that supports several business types inside a single customer experience: **restaurants, grocery stores, retail shops, and delivery services**.

Customers needed a simple way to order and track deliveries. Drivers needed reliable tools to manage runs. Store owners needed practical control over orders and sales — all within one technical foundation that could grow over time.

Local integrations were also essential: **Muamalat** for online payments and **SMS OTP** for user verification.

## The Solution

We delivered an integrated system made up of:

### Customer app

Browse stores and products, place and pay for orders, track drivers in real time, manage addresses, use coupons and promotions, and access an in-app wallet and referral system.

### Driver app

Receive and manage delivery requests, update delivery status step by step, share live location, and track earnings and withdrawals from a dedicated interface.

### Seller app

Manage products, orders, and promotions; monitor performance and sales; print receipts directly from the store.

### Central admin panel

A full control panel for customers, drivers, stores, operating zones, orders, subscriptions, commissions, and payments.

## Key Features

- Separate apps for customers, drivers, and sellers
- Central admin panel for end-to-end platform management
- Online payments via **Muamalat**
- Cash on delivery and in-app wallet
- SMS OTP verification
- Live order and driver tracking
- Multi-module architecture in one platform (restaurants, groceries, stores, delivery, and more)
- Push notifications and real-time order status updates

## Technical Challenges

The hardest part was **real-time sync** across every side of the platform. When a customer places an order, the driver, seller, and admin panel must reflect that update immediately.

We also needed a **multi-module architecture** that supports different business types in one app while keeping per-zone settings independent.

Integrating **local payment** and **SMS services** meant adapting to Libyan API requirements and checkout flows — not just enabling a generic gateway.

## Tech Stack

- **Flutter** — Customer, driver, and seller apps
- **Laravel** — REST API and admin panel
- **MySQL** — Database
- **Laravel Reverb / WebSockets** — Real-time updates
- **Firebase** — Push notifications
- **Google Maps** — Tracking and navigation
- **AWS S3** — File storage
- **Muamalat** — Local payment gateway

## Results

The project launched as a full operating system:

- 3 independent mobile apps
- Advanced central admin panel
- Integrated online payment flow
- Live driver and order tracking
- Scalable architecture for future modules

**Jibly** moved from concept to a product in daily use across **Al-Jamil**, delivering one digital experience for customers, stores, and drivers.
